Department of CSE

Shaping the Architects of the Digital Future

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at Kings Engineering College, established in 2001, is dedicated to cultivating cutting-edge engineering skills, innovation, and strong moral values. Approved by AICTE and permanently affiliated with Anna University, we prepare students for the global IT market through immersive practical training and advanced laboratories.
